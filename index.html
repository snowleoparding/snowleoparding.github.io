Featured Projects

A curated selection of projects demonstrating my ability to deliver tangible, data-driven outcomes for high-value assets.

The Keystone Building

S: A £300M new-build university facility with ambitious operational energy targets to achieve for hand-over.

T: To guide value engineering decisions and optimize complex HVAC system integration for the overall project.

A: Led the detailed HVAC modelling, developing a unique 5-tier natural and mechanical ventilation strategy.

R: Using the model for value engineering decisions; millions in CAPEX savings with simulation-backed data.

Royal Institute of British Architects (RIBA)

S: A £20M landmark refurbishment project aiming for a significant, market-leading carbon emissions reduction.

T: To develop a robust, data-driven MEP strategy to inform the client's long-term investment plan.

A: Created a granular HVAC dynamic thermal model, simulating 31 distinct AHUs and MVHRs with proposed plant.

R: Enabled a projected 60% operational energy reduction, helping to decarbonize this important London asset.

5 St Philips Place

S: Repositioning a prime commercial asset to attract premium tenants in a highly competitive local market.

T: To secure both high-performance (NABERS) and occupant wellbeing (WELL) building certifications to drive asset value.

A: Led integrated modelling analysis, developing strategies that balanced the competing demands of energy and health.

R: Secured UK NABERS 4 Stars (DfP) & WELL Gold, maximising asset appeal and potentially increasing rental value.

The Royal Institution of Great Britain

S: A Grade I listed historic building requiring a sensitive but effective decarbonisation pathway strategy.

T: To provide a clear, modelling data-driven business case for a major HVAC plant refurbishment capital project.

A: Developed a calibrated energy model and simulated various plant replacement options.

R: Enabled a confident, simulation informed capital allocation decision that balanced heritage with performance.

Holywell Yard, London

S: A new-build commercial office aiming for market-leading ESG credentials in the Shoreditch, London.

T: To significantly surpass mandatory building regulations for energy and carbon performance targets.

A: Guided the performance modelling from early design stages to integrate high-efficiency systems and flag potential risks.

R: The final design is projected to surpass the stringent Part L2 2021 regulations by over 35%.

Northumberland College

S: A new DfE-funded college with a mandate to be a sustainability exemplar for the UK education sector.

T: To achieve Net Zero Carbon in Operation (NZCiO) from the very first year of the building's official opening.

A: Drove the entire NZCiO modelling strategy, leading the detailed performance modelling design efforts.

R: Simulation tested design expected to be fully compliant with the ambitious NZCiO target, setting a new benchmark.
