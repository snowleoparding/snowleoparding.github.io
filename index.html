Selected Case Studies

One flagship project followed by six concise summaries. Images can link to detailed reports or Google Drive previews.

Keystone Building, University of Glasgow

Whole-building performance model for a major teaching and research facility. Detailed ApHVAC with TM54/TM63; mixed-mode ventilation and night-cooling strategies; Section 6 compliance. Developed calibrated control logic to align architectural intent, mechanical design and operations, and delivered off-axis sensitivity analysis to support value-engineering decisions and capital planning.

Open report Download summary

RIBA HQ, 66 Portland Place, London

Heritage refurbishment. Energy and comfort modelling for events, gallery, library, catering and office zones. Calibrated the existing model, developed comparative plant scenarios and supported the MEP strategy with early-stage HVAC modelling and coordination. (Not a DfP project.)

Open report

The Royal Institution of Great Britain, London

Performance and decarbonisation study for a historic building. Options appraisal for HVAC and envelope interventions, with modelling and programme integration alongside the MEP team.

Open report

Holywell Yard, Shoreditch

New commercial office development targeting BREEAM Excellent and WELL Core. Operational-energy targeting and façade/load optimisation informed early-stage decisions and performance-risk management.

Open summary

5 St Philip’s Place, Birmingham

Office refurbishment with DfP-style sensitivity analysis and comfort validation (Hea04). Off-axis scenarios (e.g., heat-recovery failure, DCV off, extended hours) supported independent design review and a credible operational-performance pathway.

19 Cornwall Street

Existing office refurbishment. TM54 operational-energy lines, comfort checks and a pragmatic upgrade path towards improved EPC and operational performance.

Open summary

DfE Schools Portfolio

BB101/TM52 assessments and compliance for multiple schools. Comfort-risk mitigation through purge and night-cooling, with envelope and controls tuning to meet NZCiO and EUI objectives.

About
